# ProofBase

A decentralized education platform built on the blockchain, where tutors can create courses and exams, and students can enroll, take exams, and have their results permanently recorded on-chain.

## What it does

**For tutors**
- Create and manage courses
- Build multi-choice exams with questions, options, and correct answers attached to their courses

**For students**
- Browse and enroll in available courses
- Take exams and have scores recorded on the blockchain
- Review completed exams with a full answer breakdown

## How it works

Users connect their wallet and register as either a **Tutor** or a **Student**. All activity — course creation, enrollment, exam submissions, and scores — is stored on-chain, making results tamper-proof and verifiable.
